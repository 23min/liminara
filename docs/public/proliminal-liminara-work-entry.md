## Liminara

**Provenance infrastructure for the "prove it" era**

2024–ongoing | Open source (Apache 2.0) | Elixir/OTP

The EU is rolling out regulations between now and 2029 that share a quiet demand: don't just tell us what happened — prove it. Traceable supply chains. Tamper-evident audit trails. [Digital Product Passports](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32024R1781). Verifiable emissions calculations.

The enterprise platforms were built for the reporting era. The infrastructure for provenance-based accountability doesn't exist yet.

Liminara is an open-source runtime that makes any computation reproducible, auditable, and verifiable. It records every nondeterministic choice — an LLM response, a human approval, an applicability judgment — as an immutable, content-addressed decision record. The result: cryptographically sealed evidence trails where every output can be traced to its source data through every calculation and decision along the way.

Built on Elixir/OTP. Five concepts. One scheduler loop. Zero external dependencies for the core.

Currently exploring applications in EU regulatory compliance — [Digital Product Passports](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32024R1781), [VSME](https://www.efrag.org/en/projects/vsme-standard/concluded) sustainability reporting for SMEs, supply chain transparency under [EUDR](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32023R1115). Looking for domain expertise and early collaborators.
